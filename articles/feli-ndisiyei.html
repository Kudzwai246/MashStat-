Feli Nandi’s Ndisiyei Climbs to #2 on MashA50 Chart

Harare, Zimbabwe — April 17, 2025

Zimbabwean Afro‑fusion artist Feli Nandi has surged to the #2 spot on this week’s MashA50 album chart with her latest record Ndisiyei. Since its release, the album’s blend of soulful vocals, poignant songwriting, and rich traditional rhythms has resonated deeply with fans across digital platforms.

Ndisiyei trails only Winky D’s chart‑topping Pabho, and sits just ahead of Saintfloew’s Trap Yevafundisi at #3, rounding out a powerful top three that showcases the depth and diversity of Zimbabwean music today.

MashA50 is updated every Wednesday by MashStat, ranking the 50 most streamed albums in Zimbabwe based on aggregated data from YouTube, Spotify, and Apple Music. Feli Nandi’s rise to #2 reflects her growing influence in the Afro‑fusion scene and the strong connection her music has forged with listeners nationwide.
