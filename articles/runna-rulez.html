Runna Rulez: The Rap Maverick Who’s Already Here

Harare, Zimbabwe — April 17, 2025

When Bling 4 and Runna Rulez released “Murume” together in late 2024, they didn’t just drop a track—they announced a new era for Zimbabwean hip‑hop. Though officially a Bling 4 single, Runna Rulez’s dynamic performance and signature wordplay make it a true co‑creation.

In under a year, Runna Rulez has risen from online freestyles to commanding stages that draw impressive crowds across Harare and beyond. “Murume” has accumulated thousands of streams on YouTube, Spotify, and Apple Music, and his live shows consistently pack venues with eager fans.

While he’s yet to release a full album on the MashA 50, Runna Rulez is already chart‑topping material. His work on “Murume” proves his readiness—expect his upcoming debut LP to storm MashStat’s top albums when it arrives.
