About MashStat

MashStat is Zimbabwe’s leading music chart platform — ranking what’s hot, what’s next, and what’s timeless. Whether you're an artist pushing the culture or a fan vibing with it, we’ve got your back.

Our charts are curated by real, no-nonsense music lovers who track verified streaming data from platforms like YouTube, Spotify, and Apple Music. We dig into the numbers so you don’t have to. No bias. No manipulation. Just music, ranked right.

We're Madyavanhu Kudzwai N and Zinyama Larry M, the founders of MashStat. We started this platform because we were tired of people saying "My cousin’s song is number 1 because my aunt likes it." No shade to your aunt — but we prefer stats over family hype.

We believe every artist deserves a shot and every fan deserves the facts. MashStat is here to help Zimbabwean talent shine locally and globally, with real recognition and visibility based on performance, not popularity contests.

Thank you for being part of the musical journey with all of us at MashStat. Let’s grow the game — together.
