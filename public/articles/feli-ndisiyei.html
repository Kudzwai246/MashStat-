Feli Nandi’s Ndisiyei Shines at #2 on MashA50

Harare, Zimbabwe — April 17, 2025

Zimbabwean Afro‑fusion sensation Feli Nandi has soared to the #2 spot on this week’s MashA50 chart with her spellbinding album Ndisiyei. The record’s heartfelt lyrics, rich vocals, and seamless blend of traditional and modern sounds have captured the hearts of listeners across YouTube, Spotify, and Apple Music.

Ndisiyei follows closely behind Winky D’s chart‑topping Pabho, while Saintfloew’s Trap Yevafundisi holds #3—demonstrating the dynamic diversity of Zimbabwe’s album scene.

MashA50—updated every Wednesday by MashStat—ranks the top 50 albums in Zimbabwe based on verified streaming data. Feli Nandi’s climb to #2 highlights her growing influence in Afro‑fusion and the deep connection her music has forged with audiences nationwide.
